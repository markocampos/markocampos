## 🚀 What I'm Building

### [Loom](https://github.com/markocampos/Loom)
**Your personal analytics OS.** Track daily metrics, discover hidden correlations, and turn your habits into actionable insights.

- 📊 **Smart Tracking** — Mood, sleep, coffee, steps, screen time, and custom metrics
- 🔍 **Correlation Analysis** — Pearson analysis to find what actually affects your mood
- ⚡ **Anomaly Detection** — Get notified when something unusual happens
- 📱 **Cross-Platform** — Vue 3 + Capacitor for web and Android
- 🔒 **Privacy-First** — All data stays on your device, no accounts required

**Stack:** `Vue 3` `Pinia` `Vite` `TailwindCSS` `ECharts` `Capacitor` `Health Connect`

---
